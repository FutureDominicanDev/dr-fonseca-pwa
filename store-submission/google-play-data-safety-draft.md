## Health Apps Declaration

Declare that the app handles health or medical information for clinic care coordination. The app is a private portal for approved clinic staff and patients, not a public diagnostic or emergency-care app.

## Data Types To Disclose

Disclose data collected or processed as applicable:

- Personal info: name, phone number, email address, office/location preference.
- Health and fitness: procedure details, surgery dates, prescriptions, allergies, medications, clinical history, medical notes.
- Messages: patient/staff messages and internal staff chats.
- Photos and videos: patient uploads and staff/internal record photos or videos.
- Audio files: voice messages, audio notes, and call-related media where used.
- Files and docs: PDFs, forms, prescriptions, and patient documents.
- App activity: audit logs, access events, notification events, staff approval actions, security events.
- Device or other ids: authentication ids, push tokens, session/security identifiers.
- Diagnostics: crash or reliability data only if collected.

## Sharing And Purpose

Primary purposes:

- App functionality.
- Account management.
- Security, fraud prevention, and compliance.
- Clinic communication and care coordination.
- Legal, audit, medical, and regulatory retention.

Do not disclose advertising, sale of data, or third-party tracking unless a future integration actually introduces it.

## Security Answers

- Data is transmitted over HTTPS.
- Patient data access is restricted to authorized users.
- Pending staff are blocked until approved.
- Regular staff access is limited by assigned rooms and permissions.
- Account deletion is available by public URL and in app links, subject to medical/legal/security retention.
